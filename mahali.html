Learning Mahali

The Mahali is a sacred, traditional instrument used by Jugi (tailor caste musicians of Bhaktapur). It is a conical bore double reed shawm, the melody instrument of Navabaja. Even amongst the Jugi musicians, knowledge of the instrument is eventually lost. Ravi Kapali is the last surviving musician of his generation who can play this instrument.Night has been setting aside a certain amount of fund from the performance fee received for each concert. This fund will provide an opportunity to learn Mahali for 3 months. Up to now we are able to cater two individuals.
