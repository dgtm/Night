The Caucasian Chalk Cirlce

Night has worked collaboratively on a variety of projects with the latest exploration in theater. We collaborated with Aarohan Gurukul Theater, a leading Nepali theater group, in doing the adaptation of the German play “The Caucasian Chalk Circle” in Nepali. We composed songs and wrote music impressing people and critics alike and the play is considered to be a landmark in Nepali theater for its credentials in music and songs that created space and possibilities for more of such collaborations in the future. We traveled and toured with the group across India in putting up the adaptation for Indian audiences at Bharat Rang Mahotsav Theater Festival organized by National School of Drama in New Delhi and Chennai.
